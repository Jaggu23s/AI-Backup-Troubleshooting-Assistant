# Investigation Framework

## Purpose

The AI Backup Troubleshooting Assistant follows a structured investigation framework rather than generating immediate responses.

The objective is to ensure investigations are:

- Repeatable
- Evidence-driven
- Consistent
- Explainable

---

# Investigation Stages

## Stage 1

Receive the backup failure details.

---

## Stage 2

Identify the primary error message.

---

## Stage 3

Determine the probable issue category.

Possible categories include:

- Backup Software
- Windows
- VMware
- Storage
- Network
- Security
- Authentication
- Performance

---

## Stage 4

Determine which diagnostic modules should be executed.

---

## Stage 5

Collect supporting evidence.

---

## Stage 6

Validate collected evidence.

---

## Stage 7

Generate AI-assisted analysis.

---

## Stage 8

Calculate confidence score.

---

## Stage 9

Generate recommendations.

---

## Guiding Principles

The assistant never assumes.

Every recommendation must be supported by evidence.
