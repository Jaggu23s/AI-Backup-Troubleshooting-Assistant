# Investigation Workflow

## Step 1

Receive backup failure details.

## Step 2

Analyze the initial error message.

## Step 3

Classify the issue category.

## Step 4

Determine which diagnostic collector modules are required.

## Step 5

Collect evidence using the Unified PowerShell Collector.

## Step 6

Analyze logs using AI.

## Step 7

Identify the probable root cause.

## Step 8

Generate recommended remediation steps.

## Step 9

Produce a structured investigation report.
