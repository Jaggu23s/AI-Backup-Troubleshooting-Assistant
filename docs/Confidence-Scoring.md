# Confidence Scoring

Every investigation includes a confidence score.

## High (90–100%)

- Multiple evidence sources agree.
- Root cause confirmed.
- Knowledge Base match available.

---

## Medium (70–89%)

- Strong indicators.
- Minor evidence missing.
- Additional verification recommended.

---

## Low (Below 70%)

- Insufficient evidence.
- Conflicting logs.
- Additional diagnostics required.

---

Confidence is never based on assumptions.

It is calculated from available evidence.
